Instructions

  1. Peel and dice 3 medium potatoes and 2 medium carrots.

  2. Boil the potatoes and carrots until tender, then drain and let cool.

  3. In a separate pot, boil 1 cup of green peas for about 5 minutes, then drain.

  4. Dice 2 hard-boiled eggs and 1 medium beet (previously boiled and peeled).

  5. In a large bowl, mix the potatoes, carrots, peas, eggs, and beet.

  6. Add Â½ cup of mayonnaise, salt, and pepper to taste, then mix well.

  7. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
